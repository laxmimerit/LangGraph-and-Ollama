Query: What was Amazon's revenue in 2023?## Amazon's Revenue in 2023

Amazon's total **revenue (net sales)** for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2023, was **$574.785 billion**. This figure is derived from the consolidated net sales reported in **Document 3**, which breaks down revenue by segment:  
- **North America**: $352.828 billion (61% of total revenue)  
- **International**: $131.200 billion (23%)  
- **AWS**: $90.757 billion (16%)  

### Key Insights:
- **Year-over-Year Growth**: Total revenue increased by **12%** compared to 2022 ($513.983 billion).  
- **Segment Performance**:  
  - **North America** grew by 12%, driven by third-party sellers, advertising, and subscriptions.  
  - **International** rose 11%, aided by currency adjustments ($88 million uplift in 2023).  
  - **AWS** saw a 13% increase, reflecting strong demand for cloud services.  
- **Foreign Exchange Impact**: Currency fluctuations reduced net sales by $71 million in 2023, though this is excluded in the "excl. FX" growth metrics [3].  

### Context:
Amazon’s revenue growth was influenced by macroeconomic factors, including inflation, interest rates, and supply chain volatility, as noted in **Document 3**. The company’s focus on expanding product selection, lowering prices, and improving logistics contributed to the sales uplift [3].
