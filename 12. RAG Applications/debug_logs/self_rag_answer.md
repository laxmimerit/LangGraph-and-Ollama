Query: Compare Apple and Amazon revenue in Q1 2024## Comparison of Apple and Amazon Revenue in Q1 2024

### **Total Revenue**
- **Apple**:  
  Apple's total net sales for Q1 2024 (three months ended March 30, 2024) were **$90.75 billion** [1]. This represents a slight decline compared to Q1 2023 ($94.84 billion) [1].  
  - **Segment Breakdown**:  
    - iPhone: $45.96 billion  
    - Services: $23.87 billion  
    - Wearables, Home, and Accessories: $7.91 billion  
    - Mac, iPad, and Other: $15.02 billion  

- **Amazon**:  
  Amazon's consolidated net sales for Q1 2024 (three months ended March 31, 2024) were **$143.31 billion**, reflecting a **13% year-over-year increase** compared to $127.36 billion in Q1 2023 [2].  
  - **Segment Breakdown**:  
    - North America: $86.34 billion (60% of total)  
    - International: $31.94 billion (22%)  
    - AWS: $25.04 billion (18%)  

### **Revenue Growth and Drivers**
- **Apple**:  
  - Revenue declined slightly in Q1 2024, with the iPhone segment (Apple's largest revenue source) contributing $45.96 billion, down from $51.33 billion in Q1 2023 [1].  
  - Services revenue grew to $23.87 billion, driven by subscriptions and digital content [1].  

- **Amazon**:  
  - Revenue growth was fueled by increased unit sales across third-party sellers, advertising, and subscriptions [2].  
  - AWS (Amazon Web Services) contributed $25.04 billion, up 17% year-over-year, reflecting strong demand for cloud services [2].  

### **Key Differences**
- **Scale**: Amazon’s Q1 2024 revenue ($143.31 billion) was **58% higher** than Apple’s ($90.75 billion) [1][2].  
- **Segment Focus**:  
  - Apple’s revenue is heavily reliant on hardware (iPhone, Mac, iPad) and services.  
  - Amazon’s revenue is diversified across retail (North America, International), AWS, and third-party seller fees.  
- **Growth Trends**: Amazon demonstrated robust growth (13% YoY), while Apple’s revenue slightly declined, though its services segment showed resilience [1][2].  

### **Deferred Revenue and Recognition**
- Apple’s Q1 2024 revenue included **$3.3 billion** from deferred revenue recognized in the quarter, compared to $3.5 billion in Q1 2023 [1].  
- Amazon’s unearned revenue (prepaid services) totaled **$20.9 billion** as of December 31, 2023, with $6.1 billion recognized in Q1 2024 [2].
