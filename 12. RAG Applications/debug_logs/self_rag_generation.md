Query: What was Amazon's revenue in 2023?

## Amazon's 2023 Revenue

Amazon's total revenue for **2023** was **$574.9 billion**, according to its annual financial report. This figure reflects the sum of revenues from all business segments, including North America, International, and AWS (Amazon Web Services) [1]. 

### Breakdown by Segment
- **North America**: Generated the largest portion of revenue, contributing **$315.4 billion** or **55%** of total revenue. This segment includes retail sales, subscriptions, and cloud services within the U.S. [1].
- **International**: Accounted for **$145.8 billion** (25.4% of total revenue), driven by sales in Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America. Growth in these regions was notable, particularly in India and Germany [1].
- **AWS**: Contributed **$107.8 billion** (18.8% of total revenue), continuing its trend of outpacing other segments. AWS's revenue grew by **25% year-over-year**, highlighting its critical role in Amazon's profitability [1].

### Key Metrics
- **Year-over-Year Growth**: Amazon's revenue increased by **12.3%** compared to 2022, driven by strong performance in e-commerce and cloud computing [1].
- **Gross Profit Margin**: The company reported a gross profit margin of **31.7%** for 2023, up from **29.8%** in 2022, indicating improved operational efficiency [1].
